heals the minds, body, and spirit

The Serenity Springs philosophy is simple. We can and will recover from the hopeless state of mind, body, and spirit that results in substance abuse to mask the problems within. Our expert clinical staff teams up with our treatment professionals who have recovered themselves. Our unique and effective client to staff ratio of 2:1 is a key factor to our success with long-term sobriety.

We are determined to remain a tight-knit, “family-structured” substance abuse treatment center, which provides a very comfortable and homey atmosphere. Again, this separates us from the corporate approach to recovery that many sick and suffering addicts have been cycled through, never reaping the full benefits that recovery offers nor hearing a real solution to their pain and misery. Welcome home! It is time to be free!
